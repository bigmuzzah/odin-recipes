Yorkshire Pudding

The secret to getting gloriously puffed-up Yorkshire puddings is to have the fat sizzling hot and don't open the oven door!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Pinch of salt

  • Measure out the Flour
  • Break the eggs into the flour
  • Add the milk
  • Beat untill the mixture becomes a batter
  • Put the oil into the ramekin in the over and heat to 250 degrees untill smoking
  • Pour the batter into the ramekins and cook for 20 mins
